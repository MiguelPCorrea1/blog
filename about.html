About Me

My name is Miguel. I am 19 years old. I aim to study Molecular Biology in college because I believe that the area can provide different solutions to existing and new problems in the world.

Due to my interest and passion in Bioengineering, I decided to create and develop this blog. Some years ago, I felt it was difficult to find clear-to-understand, detailed, and accurate information about Bioengineering and lab methods. The purpose of this blog is to provide in-depth details about existing and promising technologies that can help make a difference in people’s lives.
