About the Blog

My name is Miguel. I am 19 years old. I am aiming to study Molecular Biology in college because I believe that the area can provide different solutions to existing problems as well as new problems in the world.

 

Due to my interest and passion in Bioengineering, I decided to create and develop this blog. Some years ago, I felt it was difficult to find clear-to-understand, detailed and accurate information about Bioengineering and lab methods. This blog’s purpose is to provide in-depth details about existing and future promising technologies that can help to make a difference in people’s lives.
