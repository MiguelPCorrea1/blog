Gene Therapy

Set 5, 2024

Gene therapy is a medical approach that aims to prevent and treat diseases by the use of genetic material. It adds a completely new gene, or a modified one to the person. It can even replace the genes with other genes, or it can inactivate genes. That way, gene therapy aims to get rid of a disease by replacing or inactivating a bad gene.

Cells Culture

Oct 25, 2024

Cell culture is a process of growing cells outside their natural environment, like growing human cells outside a human body. For this, it is needed to isolate the human cells of a specific tissue, nurture them, and control their environment and conditions. Those factors help the cells grow and, therefore, accelerate their development.
